Our Workflow

The Scrapbook Creation Process

From messy digital folders to a pristine physical album, we handle the heavy lifting while keeping you in creative control.

1

Consult & Upload

Book a call to discuss your vision, then securely upload your raw photo collection to our private portal.

2

Curation & Layout

Our designers filter out duplicates and arrange your best shots into a stunning, story-driven digital layout.

3

Review & Refine

Receive a digital proof. You can request unlimited revisions until every page looks exactly how you want it.

4

Print & Deliver

We send the final design to our artisan bindery for museum-grade printing and hand-assembly before shipping.

Aesthetics

Memory Book Themes

Choose a foundational layout theme, and our designers will adapt it to suit your specific photos and aesthetic preferences.

Heritage

Classic, timeless layouts with serif fonts and structured grids. Perfect for family histories and vintage photo restorations.

Minimalist

Editorial-style layouts with lots of negative space, sleek sans-serif fonts, and full-bleed image spreads for a modern look.

Whimsical

Playful layouts featuring subtle pastel backgrounds, overlapping images, and delicate script fonts. Great for baby books.
