Care Guide

Archival Care & Preservation

To ensure your custom memory books and albums remain beautiful for generations to come, follow these simple museum-grade storage and handling practices.

Climate Control

Store in cool, dry areas away from direct sunlight, heating vents, or damp basements to avoid paper deterioration.

Proper Handling

Wash and dry hands before turning pages, or wear archival cotton gloves to keep oils from smudging photographs.

Display Flat

For layflat books, store them flat rather than upright to support the spine and heavy photographic pages uniformly.
